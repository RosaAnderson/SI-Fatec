Ladies and gentlemen of the council, we need to adopt measures to improve the situation in which Papotech finds itself. We have a budget of two hundred thousand euros to invest in improvements.

We have two main problems: customer complaints and employee attrition.

My suggestions are:

- Offer ten percent discounts for the most popular products and an additional five percent discount for the best customers.

- Increase employee salaries by five percent.

- Invest in employee training to improve customer retention.

- Give three of our best employees a leadership position.

In this way, we will have an investment of two hundred thousand euros, which are:

One hundred thousand for popular products.

Forty-five thousand for the best customers.

Twenty-five thousand for the increase in employee salaries.

Ten thousand for employee training.
